MIXTEAM SPORTS

Built for the Bay Area athlete who needs a real plan.

Talent opens the door, but preparation keeps it open. We help athletes understand where they stand, what college coaches need to see, and how to build a stronger profile across sport, school, film, and communication.

Our work is practical: evaluations, recruiting calendars, academic check-ins, highlight reviews, outreach strategy, and honest family guidance from the first conversation through the next step.

The athlete pathway

Five steps from potential to college conversations.

01

Discover

We learn the athlete's goals, current level, academics, schedule, and family priorities.

02

Develop

We shape a recruiting plan with sport targets, academic requirements, and clear milestones.

03

Showcase

We help organize highlights, event choices, profiles, and coach-facing materials.

04

Connect

We support outreach and conversations with schools that match the athlete's path.

05

Commit

We help families compare offers, visits, academic fit, cost, and long-term support.
